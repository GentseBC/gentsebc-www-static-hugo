Hi members of the nicest badminton club in Ghent
Our yearly club barbecue will occur on 21 september 2024 at 16h.

<br>COZY GHENT BC BBQ 2023<br>
🥩 When: Saturday 21 september 2024 as of 4:30 PM<br>
